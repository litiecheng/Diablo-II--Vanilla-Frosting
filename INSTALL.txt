By Jonathan Vasquez (fearedbliss)
Released on Sunday, August 14, 2016
-----------------------------------------------

Installation:
-------------
1. Backup your current Diablo II directory before installing. 
   A simple copy/paste of your current Diablo II folder will do.

2. Extract and replace all the files that are included in the target version
   you want to play, and place them in your Diablo II folder.

3. Enjoy.

Uninstallation:
Copy and Replace all the files in your Diablo II directory with the ones in
the "Stock" folder.
