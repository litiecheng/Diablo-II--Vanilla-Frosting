Vanilla Frosting 8.3.0 [1.13d]
By Jonathan Vasquez (fearedbliss)
Released on Thursday, November 24, 2016
----------------------------------------

Installation:
-------------
1. Backup your current Diablo II directory before installing. 
   A simple copy/paste of your current Diablo II folder will do.

2. Copy all of the files in the "Files" folder in this mod into your
   Diablo II folder, replacing the files that are already there.

3. Enjoy.

Uninstallation:
1. Copy all of the files in the "Stock" folder in this mod into your
   Diablo II folder, replacing the files that are already there.
